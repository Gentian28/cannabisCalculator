Due to the inefficiency of butter extraction, only an average of 60% of the cannabinoids (such as THC and CBD) are extracted, so this calculation takes that into account (source).

To be sure your cannabutter is as close to possible to this estimated amount of THC or CBD, follow these recommendations:

  1. Decarboxylate (release the full potential of the psychoactive effects using heat) the cannabis.

  2. Use high quality, unsalted, organic butter to avoid additional water and foreign substitutes.

  3. Portion Vertically - If you use only a portion of the cannabutter, do not scoop off of the top. Gravity impacts the way cannabinoids settle.

  4. Mix well - be sure the mixture for your edible using the cannabutter is perfectly uniform throughout.

  5. Cut the final product evenly into the precise number of portions. Use a ruler or scale if needed.
